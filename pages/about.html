ABOUT US

Get to Know us Better

For over a decade, ENAN has been a cornerstone of reliability and expertise in the dynamic world of technology repair and sales. Established twelve years ago by the esteemed and personable entrepreneur, Lazaro Raynand F., our journey began with a singular mission: to provide unparalleled service and support to our cherished community of gadget enthusiasts.

At ENAN, we transcend the traditional notion of a repair shop; we're your dedicated partners in technology. From the moment you step into our doors, you're met with warmth and a genuine commitment to resolving your gadget-related concerns. Whether it's a cracked screen, a malfunctioning motherboard, or simply seeking advice on the latest tech trends, our team of skilled technicians is here to guide you every step of the way.

With over a decade of experience, ENAN has perfected its craft. Our comprehensive range of services spans from intricate repairs to seamless gadget sales, ensuring that whatever the issue, we have a tailored solution just for you. From smartphones and laptops to gaming consoles and beyond, there's no gadget challenge too daunting for our dedicated team to overcome.

As we embark on the next chapter of our journey, we invite you to experience the unparalleled service and expertise that defines ENAN. Join us as we continue to uphold our legacy of excellence, innovation, and unwavering commitment to your gadget needs. Welcome to ENAN, where your technology finds its home.
